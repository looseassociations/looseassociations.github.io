APRIL IS THE CRUELLEST MONTH: An illustrated riso-printed quaranzine comprised of 3 pieces - one story, one list, one dialogue. Subjects include (but are not limited to): 420, modern love, unreality and cognitive dissonance . Handbound and hellbound, conceived in the fugue state of government-mandated solitude.

Written by Emma Cheung. Printed by Duplikat Press. A6 / 32 pages / Thread-bound by hand.
