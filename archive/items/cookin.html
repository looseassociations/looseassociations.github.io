ABOUT CONTACT SHOP

COOKIN': On Monday 15 August 2022, in celebration of the opening of Young Space in London, a group of musicians - Kamasi Washington, Sampha, Saya Grey, Valentina Magaletti - met together, for the first time, to make and record an album. Loose Associations and Burley Fisher Books invited the poets Raymond Antrobus and Victoria Adukwei Bulley to write a response to this music, as it was being made.

Cookin' is the result. It was produced and printed in situ, in a limited edition of 50 copies.

Written by Raymond Antrobus and Victoria Adukwei Bulley. Published by Young Space, Burley Fisher Books and Loose Associations. A6 / 16 pages / Thread-bound by hand.
