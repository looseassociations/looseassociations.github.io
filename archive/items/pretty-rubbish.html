PRETTY RUBBISH: a photo zine on the subject of rubbish. ‘To ponder the nature of rubbish is to ponder all that is in this existence of ours. From start to finish rubbish has to end up somewhere, in someone’s mind, in someone’s eyes. The end of the chain, the start of decay.’

All photos were shot, developed and scanned by Finn Murphy.

A5 / 24 pages / Printed by us / Thread-bound by hand / Cover and Flyleaf 100% recycled paper and card / Inside PPD photo paper
