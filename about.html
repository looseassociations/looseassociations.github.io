Loose Associations is run by Finn Murphy, Emma Cheung and pals. We make things to make you smile. Our creations combine our collective skills - writing, printing, painting, drawing and taking photos.
We have worked our practice into neat little consumables available from our online shop and canalside shop, which we operate from our narrowboat. Follow our instagram for updates on location and opening times: @looseassociations.
