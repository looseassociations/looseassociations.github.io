Issue 1 of MOUTH WATER: On Food and Voyeurism. A collection of poems, stories, photographs, collages and drawings on the subject of eating and looking and eating and looking at the one looking at you. Side-eye glances whilst rolling one's tongue over a cough sweet. Measured bites of perception.

Featuring Finn Murphy, Emma Cheung, Dom Saulter, Helena Bacon, George Marigold, Sam Donagh, Paul Cheung and Coco Oinoink. A5 / 28 pages / Thread-bound by hand / 100% recycled paper and card
