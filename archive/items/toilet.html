ODE ON A PUBLIC TOILET revolves around a three-year project to create an essay film about the literary, design and gender-specific aspects of public toilets. The zine includes stills, narration and poetry from the unreleased film and a foreword and afterword that document the wider process of filmmaking. This book is about failure, remaking and change in the artistic process.

Written by Emma Cheung. Poetry by Jack Pedder. A5 / 64 pages / Handbound with Japanese 4-hole binding / Mixture of 100% recycled Colorset paper and PPD brochure paper / Printed in a limited edition of 25.
